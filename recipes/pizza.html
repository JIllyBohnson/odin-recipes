Pizza

This classic homemade pizza recipe creates a delicious crispy crust with your favorite toppings. Perfect for a weekend dinner or gathering with friends, this recipe makes enough dough for two 12-inch pizzas. The secret is letting the dough rest properly and using a very hot oven!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar in a bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, olive oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  6. Punch down the dough and divide in half. Roll out each half into a 12-inch circle.
  7. Transfer dough to a pizza pan or parchment paper. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add your desired toppings.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  10. Let cool for 5 minutes, slice, and enjoy!
